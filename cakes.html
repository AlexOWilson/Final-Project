Cakes

We specialize in cakes from scratch, handmade using the best ingredients. All cakes are frosted and decorated in buttercream, made using real butter, never shortening. We can customize any cake to fit a desired flavor and design. Cakes are available in 3" smash cakes, 6" and 9" rounds, 1/4 and 1/2 sheet cakes, and tiered cakes in 6" and 9", 9" and 12", or 6", 9" and 12". Please contact us for pricing and availability.
