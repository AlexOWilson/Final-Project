What's a MegPie?

MegPie Pastries is a small internet based bakery in Los Angeles, focusing on hand crafted pastries using high quality ingredients to bring you delicious cakes, pies, cookies, cupcakes and other gourmet treats.
We take our time in creating excellent products, and we pride ourselves in working with customers to fulfill their needs, be it birthdays, weddings, small events, or any other occasion, MegPie Pastries is happy to assist.
