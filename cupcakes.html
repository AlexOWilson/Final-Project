Cupcakes

We specialize in cupcakes from scratch, handmade using the best ingredients. All cupcakes are frosted and decorated in buttercream, made using real butter, never shortening. We can customize any cupcake to fit a desired flavor and design. Cupcakes are available in regular and mini. Please contact us for pricing and availability.
