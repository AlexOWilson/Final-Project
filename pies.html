Pies and Tarts

Our pies and tarts are made with a traditional flaky all butter crust, or a classic pressed graham cracker or cookie crust. We offer pies from traditional apple crumble, to a more exotic passion fruit custard and everything in between.We also offer savory pies like arrabbiata and mac n' cheese.Please contact us for pricing and availability.
